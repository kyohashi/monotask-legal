MonoTask Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

1. Introduction

MonoTask ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Task Data

  • Storage: All task data is stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Sync: None - we do not sync your data to any cloud service
  • Account: Not required - no user accounts or registration
  • Transmission: Your task data is never transmitted externally

2.2 Advertising Information

MonoTask uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect the following information:

  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA): Used for personalized ad delivery
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, screen size
  • Ad Interactions: Ad impressions, clicks, and engagement
  • Technical Identifiers: IP address, user agent

Note: This information is collected and processed by Google AdMob. The MonoTask developer does not have direct access to this data.

3. How We Use Information

  • Task Data: Providing app functionality (task management, calendar, focus mode)
  • Advertising ID: Delivering relevant advertisements, measuring ad performance
  • Device Information: App optimization, bug fixing

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob

MonoTask uses Google AdMob for advertisement delivery.

  • Provider: Google LLC
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Ad Serving: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

4.2 App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS 14.5+, you will see a tracking permission prompt on first launch.

  • If you allow: You will see personalized advertisements
  • If you deny: You will see general advertisements (app functionality is not affected)

5. Data Sharing

MonoTask does not share your data with third parties, except:

  • Google AdMob for advertisement delivery (see above)
  • When required by law (court orders, legal obligations)

Important: Task data is stored entirely locally and is never transmitted externally.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Control Tracking

You can control ad tracking through:

  • Method 1: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → MonoTask
  • Method 2: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Turn off Personalized Ads

6.2 Delete Data

To delete all task data:

  • MonoTask → Settings → "Clear All Data"
  • Or uninstall the app

7. Children's Privacy

MonoTask is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Data Security

  • Task data is stored only on your device
  • iOS sandboxing prevents other apps from accessing your data
  • If iCloud Backup is enabled, data is backed up according to Apple's encryption standards

9. International Data Transfers

Your task data remains on your device. Advertising data collected by Google AdMob may be transferred internationally according to Google's privacy policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Significant changes will be communicated through the app or this page.
